About Us

Yanis Support and Care Provision is a supported living organisation which offers supported accommodation to young people from the age of 16 to 25years of age in and around Norfolk. It was incorporated in 2018 and the shareholders have extensive experience in the adult health social care sector and as well as working with children with mental health issues. At Yanis Support and Care Provision we are so passionate to work with young people, see them thrive and grow up to be well mannered and very responsible individuals.
